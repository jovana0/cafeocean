Wine enhances social gatherings with its versatile flavors and styles. For our café's wine list, please check the button below.

List of wine

  1. Cabernet Sauvignon: A popular red wine known for its bold flavors of blackcurrant, blackberry, and cedar, often with firm tannins and a full-bodied profile.
  2. Chardonnay: A widely loved white wine characterized by its rich and buttery texture, with flavors of tropical fruits, vanilla, and oak often present.
  3. Merlot: A smooth and approachable red wine with flavors of ripe plums, cherries, and herbs, offering a medium-bodied and velvety palate.
  4. Pinot Grigio: A refreshing white wine with light citrus and stone fruit notes, known for its crisp acidity and versatility as a food pairing wine.
  5. Sauvignon Blanc: A vibrant white wine with zesty flavors of grapefruit, lime, and passionfruit, often displaying a lively acidity and herbal undertones.

Beer €3.00

Beer unites with diverse flavors and styles, fostering enjoyment and connections. For our café's beer list, please check the button below.

List of beers

  1. Bud Light: A popular light lager known for its crisp and refreshing taste, often enjoyed as a session beer.
  2. Coors Light: A well-known light beer that offers a clean and smooth flavor, making it a popular choice for those seeking a light and easy-drinking option.
  3. Guinness: An iconic Irish stout with a rich and creamy texture, featuring notes of roasted malt, chocolate, and coffee, enjoyed by fans of darker beers.
  4. Corona Extra: A Mexican pale lager recognized for its light and refreshing character, often served with a wedge of lime for added citrusy flavor.
  5. Heineken: A Dutch pale lager with a distinctively crisp and slightly bitter taste, widely consumed around the world and appreciated for its consistent quality.

Cocktail €3.00

Cocktails create flavorful social experiences. For our café's cocktail list, please check the button below.

List of cocktails

  1. Mojito: A refreshing cocktail made with rum, lime juice, mint leaves, sugar, and soda water, known for its vibrant and citrusy flavor profile.
  2. Margarita: A classic tequila-based cocktail featuring lime juice, triple sec, and a salt rim, offering a perfect balance of tangy and sweet flavors.
  3. Martini: A sophisticated cocktail traditionally made with gin and vermouth, served either "dry" or "dirty," renowned for its elegance and timeless appeal.
  4. Piña Colada: A tropical delight combining rum, coconut cream, and pineapple juice, delivering a creamy and fruity taste reminiscent of exotic beach destinations.
  5. Old Fashioned: A timeless bourbon-based cocktail muddled with sugar, bitters, and a twist of citrus, showcasing a harmonious blend of flavors and a hint of nostalgia.

List of grilled meats

  1. Steak: A popular choice for grilling, steak offers a range of cuts such as ribeye, sirloin, and filet mignon, known for their juicy texture and rich flavor.
